⏰ Deadlines
🕐 British Summer Time (BST) is the official clock of this game. All deadlines are measured in BST (UTC+1), the standard UK time in use during all three Grand Tours. The current BST time is verified and shown below the navigation bar at all times.
Pre-Tour Picks should be entered before 10:00 BST on 4 July. Fields do not lock. However, if any Pre-Tour field is left empty at 10:00 BST on 4 July, a flat −2 point penalty is applied to that player's total score.
Daily stage Picks must be entered before 10:00 BST on each stage day. Fields lock at the deadline. Empty fields at 10:00 BST are recorded as DNS (0 points). Late input is blocked automatically.
🃏 The Joker
Each player has one Joker for the entire tour. Tap the 🃏 button next to a stage input to activate it, it glows purple when active. Tap again to deactivate.
If your Joker pick is correct → 2 points instead of 1.
If you DNS the stage where your Joker is set → Joker is cancelled and lost.
Players may select the same riders as each other — no restrictions.
🏆 Tiebreakers
If two or more players finish level on points at the end of Le Tour, the following tiebreakers apply in order:
1 Maillot Jaune — the player who correctly picked the Tour winner wins the tie.
2 GC Podium — the player with the most correct picks across GC 1st, 2nd and 3rd place wins the tie.
3 Jerseys — the player with the most correct jersey picks (Green, Polka Dot and White — Lanterne Rouge and French Stage Wins excluded) wins the tie.
4 Stage wins — the player with the most correct daily stage picks wins the tie.
5 Tie stands — if still level after all tiebreakers, there is more than one champion. 🏆🏆
⚖️ Sanctions
WADA and UCI do not have jurisdiction over this game, nor are their rules recognised. However, this game operates its own sanctions framework aligned with the cycling calendar.
Sanctions are not applied immediately, they are pending the outcome of the relevant UCI or WADA decision process. Once a decision is reached, the following rules apply:
Giro A sanction confirmed during the Giro applies to that rider's Giro results. A sanction confirmed after the Giro but before Le Tour starts also applies to the Giro, Le Tour is the deadline. Once Le Tour has started, subsequent sanctions relating to Giro riders no longer apply.
Le Tour A sanction confirmed during Le Tour applies to that rider's Tour results. A sanction confirmed after Le Tour but before La Vuelta starts also applies to Le Tour results. Once La Vuelta has started, subsequent sanctions no longer apply.
La Vuelta A sanction confirmed during La Vuelta applies to that rider's Vuelta results. A sanction confirmed after La Vuelta but before Il Lombardia (the last Monument of the season) also applies to La Vuelta results. After Il Lombardia, subsequent La Vuelta sanctions no longer apply.
Where a sanction is upheld under these rules, points will be recalibrated accordingly for all affected players.
🛡️ Spoiler Shield
The 🛡️ Spoiler Shield button sits flush against the 👁️ All Picks tab in the navigation bar, so it is clear they are associated. Tap it to activate, when on it turns red.
Per-player — each player has their own independent shield. A player activating their shield does not affect the other players. The shield shown always reflects whoever is currently identified as themselves in the Stages tab.
When active — stage results and picks in the All Picks table are hidden from the moment of activation onwards. Past results (stages already revealed before activation) remain fully visible.
When deactivated — all results become visible simultaneously, including any stages that accumulated whilst the shield was active.
The Spoiler Shield only hides stage results in All Picks. It does not affect the Leaderboard, Points Breakdown, or Live Race Standings. Simply avoid clicking those tabs if you do not want to see them.
The shield persists across page loads and browser restarts. It remains active until the player explicitly taps the button to have it deactivated. Refreshing the page will not turn it off.
📢 Media Centre
The 📢 Media tab is a shared message board for all players.
Select your name from the dropdown, type your message, and tap Post. Messages are visible to all players in real time.
Anquetil posts are used to notify players of any changes or updates, scoring corrections, rule clarifications, technical issues etc.
🔒 Privacy & Pick Visibility
✏️A player can change their stage pick any number of times before the deadline. The most recent entry is what counts. Once the deadline passes the field locks and no further changes are possible.
🔒Before the deadline — a player's stage pick is visible only to themselves. If another player views that player's stages, they see "Pick hidden until the deadline".
🔓After the deadline — the pick is revealed to all players automatically in the Stages tab and the All Picks table.
🎽Pre-tour picks — all pre-tour selections remain hidden from other players until 10:00 on 4 July, at which point all selections are revealed simultaneously.
🚫No editing other players' picks — the app only allows a player to edit their own picks. Attempts to enter a pick under another player's name are blocked.
🏮DNS (Did Not Sign-in) — if a stage pick field is empty when the deadline passes, a DNS is recorded automatically. A DNS scores 0 points. If a Joker was active on that stage it is cancelled and cannot be used again.
